# Policy Outline Library

Use these outlines as structure, not canned prose.

## Standard Section Set

Most policies should include:
1. Purpose
2. Scope
3. Policy Statement
4. Roles and Responsibilities
5. Procedures or Control Expectations
6. Exceptions
7. Review and Approval
8. Related Evidence or Records

## Access Control Policy

Include:
- Account provisioning
- Privileged access restrictions
- MFA expectations
- Access reviews
- Offboarding and access removal

## Incident Response Policy

Include:
- Incident identification and triage
- Severity classification
- Escalation path
- Containment and recovery expectations
- Post-incident review

## Change Management Policy

Include:
- Change categories
- Review and approval expectations
- Emergency changes
- Evidence retained for changes

## Vendor Management Policy

Include:
- Vendor tiering
- Security review triggers
- Contractual or compliance review needs
- Recurring reassessment
