# Example Access Control Policy Output

## Purpose

Define how access to company systems is granted, reviewed, changed, and removed.

## Scope

Applies to employees, contractors, service accounts, and privileged users with access to systems in scope for the audit.

## Policy Statement

Access must be approved before provisioned. Privileged access must be limited to authorized personnel. Authentication controls such as MFA must be enforced for systems that hold sensitive data or administrative functions.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- System owners approve access to their systems.
- IT or Security administers identity and access tooling.
- Managers initiate onboarding and offboarding events.

## Exceptions

Exceptions must be documented, approved, and time-bound.

## Review and Approval

This section should state the actual review cadence and approver once confirmed.

## Assumptions and Missing Facts

- Review cadence for privileged access not yet confirmed.
- Identity provider and source systems not yet named.

## Evidence Hooks

- User access review record
- MFA enforcement configuration
- Offboarding tickets
- Privileged role export
