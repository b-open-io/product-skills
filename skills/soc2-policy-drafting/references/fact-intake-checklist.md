# Policy Fact Intake Checklist

Use this checklist before drafting policy language.

## Governance Facts

- Who owns the control?
- Who approves the policy?
- How often is the policy reviewed?
- How are exceptions approved and recorded?

## Operational Facts

- Which systems or teams are in scope?
- Which tools enforce the control?
- What is automated versus manual?
- What records are retained when the control operates?

## Risk Facts

- What is the policy trying to prevent?
- Which roles are privileged or high-risk?
- What happens when the control fails?

If these facts are not known, draft with placeholders and call them out after the draft.
